Personal Information

  • Account information (name, email address)

Financial Data

  • Expense records you create and share

Technical Information

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Push notification tokens
  • App usage analytics and crash reports
  • Advertising identifier (for non-premium users)
  • IP address and general location data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the SplitZen service
  • Process and track expenses and balances
  • Enable group collaboration and real-time synchronization
  • Send push notifications for app activities
  • Process premium subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Display personalized advertisements (non-premium users)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support and handle inquiries
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

With Other Users

  • Profile information within shared groups

With Service Providers

  • Google Firebase: Push notifications and analytics
  • Google AdMob: Advertising services (non-premium users)
  • Google Play Billing: In-app purchases and subscriptions

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process

Google AdMob

For non-premium users, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifier
  • Device information and usage data
  • Location data (if permitted)

You can opt out of personalized ads or remove ads entirely by upgrading to SplitZen Premium.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve our services. This includes crash reporting and performance monitoring.

5. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for:

  • New expenses added to your groups
  • Settlement requests and payment confirmations
  • Group invitations and member activities
  • Important account and security updates

You can manage notification preferences in the app settings or disable them in your device settings.

6. Premium Subscriptions and Payments

  • Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play Billing
  • We store subscription status to provide premium features
  • Payment information is handled securely by Google
  • We do not store credit card or payment details
  • You can manage subscriptions through Google Play Store

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Row-level security policies in our database
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with Supabase

8. Data Retention

Control and Correction Rights
