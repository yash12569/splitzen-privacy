SplitZen Privacy Policy

Beautiful Bill Splitting Made Simple

Last updated: December 2024

SplitZen ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application SplitZen.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • Profile information you provide
  • Contact information for group invitations

Financial Data

  • Expense records you create and share
  • Group membership and balance information
  • Settlement and payment records
  • Premium subscription status

Technical Information

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Push notification tokens
  • App usage analytics and crash reports
  • Advertising identifier (for non-premium users)
  • IP address and general location data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the SplitZen service
  • Process and track expenses and balances
  • Enable group collaboration and real-time synchronization
  • Send push notifications for app activities
  • Process premium subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Display personalized advertisements (non-premium users)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support and handle inquiries
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

With Other Users

  • Expense and balance data with group members
  • Profile information within shared groups

With Service Providers

  • Supabase: Secure data storage and real-time synchronization
  • Google Firebase: Push notifications and analytics
  • Google AdMob: Advertising services (non-premium users)
  • Google Play Billing: In-app purchases and subscriptions

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • In connection with a business transfer or acquisition

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

For non-premium users, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifier
  • Device information and usage data
  • Location data (if permitted)

You can opt out of personalized ads or remove ads entirely by upgrading to SplitZen Premium.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve our services. This includes crash reporting and performance monitoring.

5. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for:

  • New expenses added to your groups
  • Settlement requests and payment confirmations
  • Group invitations and member activities
  • Important account and security updates

You can manage notification preferences in the app settings or disable them in your device settings.

6. Premium Subscriptions and Payments

  • Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play Billing
  • We store subscription status to provide premium features
  • Payment information is handled securely by Google
  • We do not store credit card or payment details
  • You can manage subscriptions through Google Play Store

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Row-level security policies in our database
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with Supabase

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Expense data: Retained for service functionality
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 2 years
  • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Disable push notifications
  • Request data portability

10. Children's Privacy

SplitZen is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending a notification for significant changes
